Debut Movie

Pawan Kalyan made his acting debut in the Telugu film Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi released in 1996. The film was directed by E. V. V. Satyanarayana and introduced him as a new hero in the Telugu film industry. In the movie, he played a young man who falls in love with a girl from a traditional family. Although the film received a moderate response at the box office, it helped him gain recognition among audiences. His energetic acting style, unique dialogue delivery, and strong screen presence impressed viewers. Marriage life

Pawan Kalyan has had an eventful personal and family life. He first married Nandini in 1997, but their relationship ended in divorce after some years. Later, he married actress Renu Desai in 2009. The couple had two children, a son named Akira Nandan and a daughter named Aadhya Konidela. However, their marriage also ended in separation in 2012. In 2013, he married Anna Lezhneva, a Russian actress whom he met during a film shoot. They have two children together and currently live as a family. Despite challenges in his personal life, he continues to focus on his career and public service.

Achievements

Pawan Kalyan achieved great success as an actor and later as a politician. He became one of the most influential stars in Telugu cinema with blockbuster films such as Tholi Prema, Gabbar Singh, and Attarintiki Daredi. His performance in Tholi Prema earned national recognition for Telugu cinema. Known as the “Power Star,” he gained a massive fan following for his acting style and simplicity. Apart from films, he entered politics and founded the Jana Sena Party in 2014. Through this party, he aimed to serve people and bring social change. In 2024, he became the Deputy Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, marking a major milestone in his political career
